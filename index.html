Intuitive Mathematics

"I believe the search for an explanation, for understanding is what we should really be aiming for. Proof is simply part of that process, and sometimes its consequence."
— Michael Atiyah

My purpose with this website is to post intuitive (but rigorous) proofs of central theorems in mathematics. I focus on theorems for which all (or almost all) textbooks provide non-intuitive proofs.

For suggestions or comments about the articles, feel free to contact me at my email or.plevrakis@gmail.com.
